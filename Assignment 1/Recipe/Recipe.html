Jeera Rice Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 1 tablespoon ghee (clarified butter)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the basmati rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. Heat ghee in a pan over medium heat, add cumin seeds, and sauté until they splutter.
  3. Add the rinsed rice to the pan and sauté for 2-3 minutes, ensuring each grain is coated with ghee.
  4. Boil 2 cups of water and add it to the rice. Season with salt, stir gently, and bring it to a boil.
  5. Reduce the heat to low, cover the pan, and simmer for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is cooked and water is absorbed.

Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot. Enjoy your Jeera Rice!
